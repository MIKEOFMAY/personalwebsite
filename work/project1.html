Overview

A full brand identity system created using Stable Diffusion A1111 and Figma — proving that one person with AI tools can produce the output of a whole creative team. From logo concept to brand assets, all AI-assisted.

The challenge was to build a consistent, ownable visual language using generative AI — without losing the intentionality and craft that makes great brand design.

The Process

Started with extensive prompt engineering to establish a visual direction — testing hundreds of variations to find the consistent aesthetic language. Then moved into Figma to systematize everything into reusable components.

The key insight: AI doesn't replace the art director. It replaces the production time. The creative decisions — what feels right, what communicates the brand, what to throw away — that's all still human work.

Result

A complete brand system including logo variants, color palette, typography, iconography, and usage guidelines. Delivered in a fraction of the time traditional production would require — without sacrificing quality.
